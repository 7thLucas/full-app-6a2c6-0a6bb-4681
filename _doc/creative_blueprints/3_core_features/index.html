What It Does

Core Features

Pharmacy Owner Pharmacist Inventory Clerk
Drug Inventory Management

Add, update, and view stock by drug name, batch number, supplier, and quantity on hand.

Expiry Date Tracking

Automated alerts and dashboards for batches approaching or past expiry — surface risk before stock is lost.

Prescription Records

Log, search, and verify prescriptions linked to patient and drug records. Full history, always accessible.

Low-Stock Alerts

Flag items below a configurable reorder threshold before shortages affect patient service.

Audit Trail

Timestamped logs of every inventory move and prescription action. Compliance-ready, always.

Built for prevention, not recovery

Every feature exists to catch the problem before it becomes a loss — expired stock, prescription error, or empty shelf.
