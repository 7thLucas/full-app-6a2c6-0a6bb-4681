The Case for Change

The Problem

Expired Stock Losses

Drugs reaching expiry undetected become direct write-offs — money destroyed, plus patient safety and regulatory risk.

Prescription Inaccuracies

Fragmented or manual prescription records create dispensing errors, duplicate fills, and missing documentation — real liability exposure.

Invisible Inventory

Stock tracked on spreadsheets or paper means shortages and surpluses only surface after the damage is done.

The Solution

One platform that tracks every batch, flags every expiry, and locks prescription records — before the loss, not after.

  • → Real-time stock with batch-level expiry dates
  • → Automated alerts days before any expiry deadline
  • → Searchable, verified prescription records per patient
  • → Full audit trail for regulatory compliance
