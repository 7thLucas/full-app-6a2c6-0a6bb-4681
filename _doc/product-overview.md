# Product Overview — Pharmacy Inventory & Prescription Manager

> Working title: **PharmaCare Manager** (final name confirmed during onboarding)

---

## What It Is

A web-based operational management app for pharmacy owners that centralises drug inventory tracking, expiry date monitoring, and prescription record-keeping in one place. The product's mission: eliminate expired-stock write-offs and close the loop on prescription accuracy — before the loss, not after.

---

## Target Users

**Primary**: Pharmacy owners running independent or small-chain pharmacies who are currently tracking stock and prescriptions manually (spreadsheets, paper, ad-hoc systems).

**Secondary**: Pharmacists and dispensing staff who perform the day-to-day stock checks, prescription logging, and batch reviews.

---

## The Problems It Solves

1. **Expired stock losses** — drugs reaching their expiry date undetected result in direct inventory write-offs, regulatory risk, and potential patient safety incidents.
2. **Prescription inaccuracies** — manual or fragmented prescription records create the risk of dispensing errors, duplicate fills, and lost documentation, exposing the pharmacy to liability.
3. **Inventory blind spots** — without a centralised view, stock levels, batch details, and reorder points are tracked informally, making shortages and surpluses invisible until too late.

---

## Core Features

- **Drug inventory management** — add, update, and view stock by drug name, batch number, supplier, and quantity on hand.
- **Expiry date tracking** — automated alerts and dashboards for drug batches approaching or past their expiry date.
- **Prescription records** — log, search, and verify prescriptions linked to patient and drug records; full history per patient and per drug.
- **Low-stock alerts** — flag items below a configurable reorder threshold before shortages affect service.
- **Audit trail** — timestamped logs of all inventory movements and prescription actions for regulatory compliance.

---

## Positioning

Practical, pharmacy-first operational software — not a POS, not an ERP. Lightweight enough for a single-location pharmacy to adopt immediately; structured enough to prevent the two costliest operational errors: expired stock and prescription mistakes.

---

## Brand & Tone

Professional, clinical precision with approachable usability. Design palette: clean medical tones — deep teal as the primary, white backgrounds, rose/red for expiry warnings and alerts. Tone: calm, competent, no-nonsense. Pharmacy-owner-facing, not tech-jargon-facing.

---

## Strategic Principles

1. **Prevention over recovery** — surface risk before it becomes a loss.
2. **Zero friction for daily ops** — stock updates and prescription logs must be fast; friction drives workarounds.
3. **Auditability by default** — every action logged; regulatory readiness is a feature, not an afterthought.

---

## Scope (MVP)

- Single-location pharmacy
- Web app (desktop-primary, pharmacy counter context)
- Core modules: Inventory, Expiry Alerts, Prescription Records, Low-Stock Alerts, Audit Log
- Out of scope (v1): POS integration, multi-location, patient billing, insurance claims
